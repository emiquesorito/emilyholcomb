Driving Exceptional Growth at JTV: Innovation and Customer Satisfaction

At JTV, we faced our fair share of challenges: an ultra-competitive home shopping and e-commerce market, evolving consumer expectations, and the need to constantly innovate. My role was all about turning these challenges into opportunities. By focusing on enhancing customer engagement, driving digital transformation, and optimizing operations, we aimed to not just keep up, but lead the pack.

Expanding Our Reach with OTT Platforms

I realized we could do more to reach our audience, especially those cutting the cord on cable. So, we brought JTV Live to LG and Samsung smart TVs, widening our accessibility and tapping into a larger market. This strategic move paid off, adding about $0.5 million in sales from these platforms alone.

Boosting Conversions with UX Overhauls

Improving app performance was another area I dove into. From speeding up load times to introducing QR codes for instant product access during shows, these changes led to a 12% jump in customer conversions. It's amazing how small tweaks can lead to big wins in engagement and sales.

Looking Ahead
